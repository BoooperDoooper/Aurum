The Aurum Story

Founded in 2018 by master chocolatier Elizabeth Moreau, Aurum was born from a passion for creating exquisite chocolate experiences that delight all the senses.

Each Aurum bonbon is handcrafted in our atelier using only the finest ingredients sourced from around the world. We work directly with cacao farmers to ensure sustainable practices and exceptional quality.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond taste to the visual artistry of each piece. Every Aurum creation is a miniature masterpiece, designed to bring luxury and joy to chocolate lovers everywhere.

Our Premium Ingredients

We source only the finest ingredients from around the world to create our exceptional chocolates.

Single-Origin Cacao

Ethically sourced from small farms in Venezuela, Madagascar, and Ecuador for distinct flavor profiles.

Natural Flavors

We use only natural extracts and fresh ingredients for authentic and vibrant flavors in every bite.

Sustainable Practices

Our ingredients are responsibly sourced with a commitment to environmental stewardship and fair trade.
